The Problem We Solve

Features

Rent Payment

Allows residents to make rent and utility payments via web or app. Also allows residents to pay each other back via web or app electronically. 

Package Logging

Notifies residents of packages being held in the office in real time. Our system keeps a log of packages coming in and out of the office, whether they are damaged, when they were picked up, ect.

Social Shopping

Social Shopping facilitates the introduction between roommates and allows them to collaborate on household goods & appliances they may have or need to purchase.

Notifications Center

Allows management to notify single residents, units, and entire communities. Notify specific residents of overdue rent, utility overages, when maintenance request are started and finished, upcoming events in the community, security alerts.

Roommate Matching

New tenants complete personality questionnaires when setting up individual profiles on the platform. The system puts all candidates in subgroups based on common interest and living habits, then gives property managers suggested roommates.

Maintence Request

Allows residents to submit maintenance request at the comfort of their home. Gives management insight on most common maintenance request, prioritizes emergency request, and tracks progression of tickets.
